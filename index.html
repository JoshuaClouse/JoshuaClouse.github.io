Projects

Wacktory

A VR game combined with a couch co-op experience, Wacktory is a fast paced game that forces players to work together in order to fill all their orders on time. With a diverse team of 13 memebers coming from varying backgrounds and roles, I took the lead on developing and implementing the core gameplay loop in addition to contributing pivotal game design decisions which greatly improved user experience. In addition to the gameplay loop, I was also responsible for implementing the UI in the main menu. On top of my duties as a core developer, I also composed the music for this game. If you would like to play Wacktory you can find it here on Steam.


Phonote

Phonote is an app created in Android Studio by me and 4 other collaborators. It aims to help students take notes easier by converting images to text and autogenerating citation info. Users can either take a picture in the app, or use a picture from their gallery to convert into text. A book title is all that is needed to automatically generate citation info. Users can also save their projects to google drive. Development on the project has stopped and the app is not available on Google Play, however a video showing all its features can be seen here.
